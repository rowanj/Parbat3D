Our client is Arko Lucieer

Arko's background is in physical geography with a specialisation in Geographical Information Science (GIS) and remote sensing. In 2004 he finished his PhD on uncertainty in remote sensing image classification and segmentation, and visualisation of uncertainty. Currently, he is a lecturer in GIS and remote sensing in the Centre for Spatial Information Science, University of Tasmania, Hobart.

Arko's current interest is in classification of subantarctic vegetation of Heard Island and Macquarie Island based on high-resolution satellite imagery. More information on Arko Lucieer can be found at http://www.lucieer.net
