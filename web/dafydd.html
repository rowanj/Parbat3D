Dafydd Williams

Back

Dave was born and raised in Hobart. Interested in computers from an early age, he wrote his first program at age 7.

He first attended the University of Tasmania in 1997-98, but left to create Escape Velocity NOVA, a computer game published by Ambrosia Software.

He has worked at the Friends School as a computer technician, as a help-desk operator at the University of Tasmania, and as a Software Testing Engineer at Dytech Solutions. As of this writing, he expects to complete his Bachelor of Computing in December 2006.

Dave is the treasurer of ATMOS Software Productions Pty Ltd, a member of the Golden Key Honour Society and is on the Dean's Role of Excellence for the Faculty of Science, Engineering and Technology. He is also the librarian of the TUU Anime Society, and is an active participant in the organisation of the Anime Island convention (AI-Con).

Dave is interested in old and/or unusual cars (and computers), unique and cool engineering feats, reading & creative writing, tai chi and all manner of computer games.
