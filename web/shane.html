Shane Kingston

My main interest is computer programming, which I first began while I was in primary school. The majority of my computing skills have been self-taught. I have skills in multiple different languages including assembly, c, c++, PHP and Java. I also have interests in web development, electronics and microcontroller programming.

While studying at uni I have received a couple of offers to join Golden Key International, which was only sent out to the top 15% of students based on results. I have also been added to the Dean's Role of Excellence for the Bachelor Of Computing.

I have learned a lot more about software development and have developed interests in graphics and game programming.

I have been providing website design/modification, programming and basic IT support to local businesses for the last few years. I have also completed small freelance programming and web-based jobs through the internet for people living in the mainland of Australia and the United States.

I would like to pursue a career in software development, while continuing other interests in my spare time, with the possibility of developing games in the future.
