Parbat3D is an open-source standalone application written in C++.

Features include:

  • Support for many image types such as JPEG, JPEG2000, GeoTIFF and ECW. Satellite images can be several gigabytes in size.
  • Fast display of satellite image band values under the cursor's current location.
  • Ability to create Regions Of Interest (ROIs) that can consist of many disjoint entities. Entities may be points, rectangles or polygons.
  • Ability to visualise three bands of the image colour composite in an interactive 3D scatterplot known as a Feature Space.
  • Ability to visualise the properties of selected ROIs as 3D clusters in the 3D plot to visually explore class clusters.
  • Contrast and brightness adjustment of images.
